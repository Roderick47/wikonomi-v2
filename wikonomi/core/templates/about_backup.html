Wikonomi: Price Wisdom for PNG

In the land of a thousand cultures and endless markets, where every kina matters for our families, Wikonomi is Papua New Guinea's digital marketplace guardian. We unite communities to share price knowledge, protecting hard-earned money and ensuring no family overpays for their daily needs.

Our PNG Story

From Port Moresby to Mount Hagen, from Lae to Madang, we know the struggle. Every week, our mothers and fathers walk through markets comparing prices of rice, sugar, and tin fish. Every kina saved means another meal for the children, another school book, another contribution to the wantok system.

Wikonomi transforms this age-old wisdom into digital power. When you spot a good price at TST or RH, your knowledge instantly helps thousands of other Papua New Guinean families make smarter choices. Together, we build a shield against price exploitation.

Tools for Every PNG Family

GPS Price Mapping

Find the cheapest prices within walking distance of your home. Whether you're in Boroko, Waigani, or Gerehu, instantly see which nearby shops offer the best deals on your family's essentials.

Smart Shopping Lists

Build your family's shopping list and watch the total calculate automatically based on real community prices. Never be surprised at the checkout again - know exactly what you'll spend before you leave home.

Price Watch & Alerts

Waiting for prices to drop? Watch any product and get instant notifications when someone finds it cheaper. Perfect for timing big purchases like electronics or bulk food items.

Photo Proof System

Snap photos of price tags for proof. Our smart system compresses images to save your data, so you can verify deals without worrying about phone credit costs.

Bulk CSV Upload

Shop owners and market vendors can upload hundreds of prices at once using Excel spreadsheets. Perfect for businesses wanting to share their complete inventory with the community.

Community Protection

Our community voting system removes fake or outdated prices. Together, we keep the information accurate and trustworthy for everyone's benefit.
