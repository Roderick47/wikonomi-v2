What is Wikonomi?

Wikonomi is Papua New Guinea's first community-driven price tracking platform. We empower everyday consumers by making cost of living transparent, accessible, and easily comparable across different businesses and locations.

Price Comparison

Compare prices across different stores and locations to find the best deals on everyday items.

Location-Based

Find prices near your location with interactive maps and distance-based search.

Community Driven

Real people sharing real prices from actual stores across Papua New Guinea.

Price History

Track price trends over time to know when to buy and when to wait.

Shopping Lists

Create and manage shopping lists with price estimates for budget planning.

Bulk Upload

Business owners can upload multiple prices at once using CSV files.
