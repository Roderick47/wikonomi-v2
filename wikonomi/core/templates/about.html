What is Wikonomi?

Wikonomi is Papua New Guinea’s first community-driven price tracking platform. We empower everyday consumers by making the cost of living transparent, accessible, and easily comparable across different businesses and locations.

How It Works

Prices in stores are constantly changing, and it’s hard for one person to track where the actual best deals are. Wikonomi solves this through the power of crowdsourcing.

Whenever you see a product on a shelf—whether it’s cheaper than normal or outrageously expensive—you can instantly snap a photo and record the price on Wikonomi. Instantly, thousands of other users across your city can see it and compare it to their nearby stores!

Core Features & Tools

Live GPS Mapping

Every price report allows you to tag the exact GPS location of the business. You can check the map to find the cheapest bread, rice, or electronics within walking or driving distance of your exact current location.

Smart Shopping Center

See a good deal on the feed? Click "Add to List". Your personalized shopping cart actively tracks what you need to buy and actually estimates your checkout total before you even reach the counter based on the most recent community prices.

Price Watch & Alerts

Want to buy a TV but waiting for a drop? Hit the "Watch" button on any product. Whenever another user reports that product at a new price, you'll immediately receive a notification alert in your dashboard keeping you in the loop!

Photo Verification

To maintain trust and accuracy, users can snap and upload photos of price tags. Our system compresses images locally on your phone to save your data plan, and stores them for anyone who wants visual proof of a deal.
