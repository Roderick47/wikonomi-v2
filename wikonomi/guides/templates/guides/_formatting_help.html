Formatting your guide

Text keeps its line breaks and spacing. Press Enter once for a new line, or leave a blank line for a new paragraph.

  • **bold text** makes bold text
  • *italic text* makes italic text
  • - item or 1. item creates a list
  • [label](https://example.com) creates a link
  • `short code` formats short code or numbers
  • > note creates a highlighted quote
